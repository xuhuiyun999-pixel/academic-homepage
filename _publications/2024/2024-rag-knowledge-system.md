abstract: >-
  An enterprise-style intelligent knowledge base system integrating hybrid retrieval with LLM-powered question answering. Led a three-member team to develop a campus knowledge assistant integrating papers, course materials, and administrative documents into a unified RAG platform. Built an automated data pipeline to collect and clean over 2.1GB of knowledge data, then implemented hybrid retrieval using Elasticsearch and FAISS. Fine-tuned Qwen-7B for educational scenarios and designed prompt engineering strategies to reduce hallucinations.
